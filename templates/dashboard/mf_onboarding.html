Welcome to Mentor Facilitator Onboarding

As a mentor facilitator, you are responsible for onboarding, training, and supporting mentors assigned to you. Use the resources below to help mentors get started and succeed.

Tip: Share these resources with new mentors and schedule an onboarding session.

Mentor Onboarding Checklist

  • Review mentor guidelines and code of conduct
  • Complete platform tutorial (video walkthrough)
  • Set up mentor profile with photo, bio, expertise
  • Define availability and session preferences
  • Attend safety & compliance training
  • Schedule first check‑in with facilitator

Communication Templates

Use these templates when communicating with mentors:

Welcome Email 
Hi [Mentor Name], welcome to MentorConnect! We're excited to have you on board. Please complete your profile and review the mentor guidelines. Let me know if you have any questions.
Session Reminder 
Hi [Mentor Name], a reminder that you have a session with [Student Name] on [Date]. Please ensure you're prepared and have reviewed the session plan.
Feedback Request 
Hi [Mentor Name], how was your recent session with [Student Name]? Any challenges or successes you'd like to share? Let's schedule a quick check‑in.
