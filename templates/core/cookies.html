1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

{{ SITE_NAME }} uses cookies for the following purposes:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

  • User authentication and session management
  • Security features
  • Remembering your preferences

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our services:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Time spent on pages
  • Error tracking

2.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Language preferences
  • Accessibility settings
  • User interface customizations

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

4.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Delete individual or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies

4.2 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Essential cookies are required for the platform to work properly.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, such as:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media plugins for sharing features

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.
