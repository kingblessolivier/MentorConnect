How Does It Work?

Your journey to discovering your dream profession in 6 simple steps

STEP 1

Discover

Find a professional in the field that interests you. Browse our extensive network of mentors.

STEP 2

Request

Submit your application for an observation internship with the professional of your choice.

STEP 3

Get Confirmed

Receive confirmation once approved. Get all practical information you need.

No, you're there to observe and learn, always under the guidance of your mentor.
